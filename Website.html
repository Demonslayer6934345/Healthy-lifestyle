Nutrition is the study of how food affects our bodies, and a healthy diet should include different types of nutrients. These include macronutrients like carbs, protein, and fats, and micronutrients like vitamins and minerals. Drinking enough water, eating the right amount, timing meals, and choosing healthy foods are also important. Eating healthy can have many benefits for the body and mind, so teenagers should try to eat a variety of healthy foods and avoid junk food.

Physical activity is any movement that requires energy expenditure and there are different types, each with unique benefits. Aerobic exercise, such as jogging or cycling, improves cardiovascular health and reduces the risk of chronic diseases. Strength training, like weightlifting, increases muscle strength and endurance, while flexibility exercises, like yoga, improve posture. Balance exercises, such as standing on one foot, can help prevent falls. Regular physical activity is essential for both physical and mental health, especially for teenagers who can benefit from strong bones and muscles
